ALUMNI ASSOCIATION AND MEETS | USICT


The students of Gautam Buddha University have been recruited in several National and International organisations and have proved to be a great asset to the organizations they joined. Our present graduates and post graduate would assuredly maintain the same level of performance and take their recruiting organizations to new heights in their respective fields. We highly value our partnership with alumni of Gautam Buddha University and remain committed to provide a productive and positive experience. We heartily invite the alumni organizations and graduating students to find the best match between their needs and capabilities. We are available for any requisite assistance & would ensure a positive & productive experience. Feedback & queries would be welcome and thoroughly entertained and suggestions would be appreciated.
